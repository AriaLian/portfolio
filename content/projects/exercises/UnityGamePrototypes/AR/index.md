This is a multiplayer game requiring cooperation of one player on PC and the other on iOS with AR support.

The PC player is in charge of building up defenses and piloting a mech while the AR player flies a fighter plane to provide additional ranged support. 

Together the two must defend their settlement from waves of invading aliens while strengthening their defense systems.
