Hex Chronicles: Athera is a turn-based strategy board game with unique characters, dynamic environmental tiles, and elemental interactions. This game offers a dynamic and immersive gaming experience that transports players into a fantasy world filled with adventure, and strategic depth. 

## Game Features

- Turn-based strategy gameplay set in a mystical medieval fantasy world.
- Unique environmental tiles and weather system that dynamically affect gameplay.
- Diverse range of hero classes and types with unique abilities and elemental affinities.
- Hero customization to tailor the player's experience.
- Tactical battles that require strategic thinking and adaptability.

Worked as: Project Director, Programmer, and Technical Artist
